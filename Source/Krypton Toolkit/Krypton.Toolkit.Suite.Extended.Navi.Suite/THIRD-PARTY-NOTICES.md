## Guifreaks NaviSuite — MIT

The `Navi*` control family in this module (NaviControl, NaviBand, NaviGroup,
NaviBandPopup, renderers, etc.) is derived from **Guifreaks NaviSuite**, the
Outlook-style navigation pane control for Windows Forms by Jacob Mesu:

- **Origin:** <https://github.com/jacobmesu/Guifreaks-Navisuite> (formerly
  guifreaks.net); presented in the CodeProject article "A Serious Outlook Style
  Navigation Pane Control" by the same author.
- **Licence:** MIT — Copyright (c) Jacob Mesu / Guifreaks. The repository states
  "The control is available under MIT license".
- **Modifications:** namespaces rewritten to
  `Krypton.Toolkit.Suite.Extended.Navi.Suite`, rendering and theming adapted to
  Krypton palettes.
